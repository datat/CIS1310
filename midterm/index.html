Family Travel Starts at Home

Our family travels always start from home (where else?). The photo below is the entryway to our home. As with most people, we love nature; that's why we travel. But when we are not traveling, we like home to be a place we like to be, even before we think about a change of scenery.

We live in a condo. The association does a nice job of landscaping but we can embellish it with a few perennials of our own and in the Spring we add a few annuals that keep the area bright and inviting.

My wife and I travel together, mostly shorter trips and then take longer trips with the children and grandchildren so we can impart our values, specifically our appreciation for creation into them. Nature itself provides an unparalleled touch with the Creator, especially important for the developing child!
