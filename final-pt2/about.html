My goal in my soap business is to provide handcrafted (cold process) soaps for men; nevertheless, ladies you are going to love them too. I use all the soaps I make.

Chiave soaps will be made with high quality ingredients that are 100% natural while using essential oils that are 100% pure. Chiave will provide a soap that will enhance, help & promote a healthier skin care routine for my customers.

In addition, Chiave’s customers will be able to customize their soaps to meet their skin care needs (stay tuned for more about how to order).

I want my audience to become more aware about the soaps they are using. Most of us have learned to pay attention to what we put in our bodies, but often don’t pay attention to what we put ON our bodies.

When I make soaps, that is something that is my priority…my soaps will be ones that my audience’s bodies will benefit from.

I am happy you are here with me on this journey.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create products that are of the highest quality while also being ethical, eco-friendly, and health-conscious.

Our Products

Plant-based

We use only plant-derived ingredients in our products. You don’t need to be vegan to love our soap, but if you are, then this is the soap for you.

Cruelty-free

We don’t test our products on animals.

Handcrafted

Every one of our products is made by hand in small batches.

Sustainably sourced

We source our palm oil from members of the Roundtable for Sustainable Palm Oil (rspo.org), which has strict standards that protect forests, wildlife, and indigenous people while minimizing pollution and providing fair working conditions and wages to farmers. Every ounce of oil is traceable to the farm where it was grown. Woodberry is dedicated to palm oil as a high-quality ingredient and to ensuring every drop is sustainably and ethically produced.

Free of sulfate, phthalate, paraben, and detergent

What is this stuff anyway? These harmful chemicals have no place in good soap, which is why you won’t find them at Chiave.

Scented with pure essential Oils

We only use essential oils to scent our products. These are steam-distilled pure plant extracts, meaning that they come from the ground and not from a test tube. The plants are minimally processed to release their pure essence, and retain the natural smell and flavor of their source. We never use fragrance oils, which are highly processed products that can contain synthetic chemicals that can irritate the skin and pose certain health risks.

Colored by Nature

Our soaps are colored with a variety of clays, activated charcoal, plant pigments, and natural minerals. Some of our essential oils can also affect the color of the soap.

Recyclable

More than one billion plastic body-wash bottles enter the waste system each year. With our biodegradable and plastic-free packaging and bar soaps, we are glad to help you reduce your dependence on single-use plastics.
