Policy Information

Return Policy

Our policy lasts 30 days. If 30 days have gone by since your purchase, unfortunately we can’t offer you a refund or exchange.

We guarantee 100% Satisfaction on all items. If for any reason you are not satisfied with your purchase of our soap within the 30 day window, return your product for a full refund.

To complete your return, we require a receipt or proof of purchase.

Shipping Policy

All orders placed Monday through Friday are shipped out within 48 hours via UPS or USPS Priority Mail. Orders placed Saturday or Sunday will be shipped out within 72 hours. For orders under $45 shipping is $8. For orders over $45 shipping is free. We ship to all U.S states and territories.
